## Common Rejection Reasons & Solutions

### Metadata Rejection
**Issue**: Keywords stuffing or misleading description
**Solution**: Use natural language, accurate descriptions

### Privacy Policy
**Issue**: Missing or inadequate privacy policy
**Solution**: Use the template above, host on permanent URL

### Functionality
**Issue**: App crashes or features don't work
**Solution**: Test thoroughly on multiple devices before submission

### Voice Control
**Issue**: Reviewer can't get voice control to work
**Solution**: Provide clear instructions in "Notes for Reviewer"
